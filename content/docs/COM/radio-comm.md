## Contact Procedures
- Initial Contact
	- Name of the facility being called
	- Your full aircraft ID as filed in flight plan
	- State of position
	- Message to follow or your request
>  Mather Tower, EVASTAR801, hold short RWY222R at B, ready for departure, request left crosswind departure.

- Do not select frequency without acknowledgement.
- Do not delay frequency change unless instructed.
- Call sign should NEVER abbreviate on initial contact. Once connected, may use the last 3 digits.
- Student pilots may state yourself as "student pilot"
>  Mather tower, EVASTAR 801, hold station RWY22R, student pilot solo.

### DEPARTING METHAR
COM2 · 133.3 — EVA Operations

Pilot: EVA Operations, EVASTAR 805, block out 35, full tank

EVA Operations: EVASTAR 805, block out 35, full tank, have a nice flight

Released brakes · Taxiing out of T-shed via T1

Pilot: EVA Traffic, EVASTAR 805, taxiing out of T-shed via T1

Hold short Taxiway A at T1

Switches to COM1 · 121.85 — Mather Ground

### Taxi Clearance
Pilot: Mather Ground, EVASTAR 805, hold short taxiway A at T1, ready to taxi with Lima, request local area number 

Mather Ground: EVASTAR 805, taxi Runway 22R via A B, squawk 0365

Pilot: Taxi Runway 22R via A B, squawk 0365, EVASTAR 805

Squawked 0365 

Pilot taxis to the runup area via A B, completes the runup and before takeoff check checklist, then proceeds to hold short Runway 22R at taxiway B.

### Takeoff Clearance
COM1 · 120.65 — Mather Tower

Pilot: Mather Tower, EVASTAR 805, hold short Runway 22R at B, ready for departure

Mather Tower: EVASTAR 805, Runway 22R cleared for takeoff

Pilot: Runway 22R cleared for takeoff, EVASTAR 805

Pilot takes off
