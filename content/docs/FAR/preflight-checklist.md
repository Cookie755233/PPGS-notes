## P - PILOT
### S.M.I.L.E (Document)
- **S**tudent pilot certificate - §61.3(a)
- **M**edical - §61.3(c)
- **I**D (passport) - §61.3(a)(2)
- **L**ogbook - §61.51(i)
- **E**ndorsement (within logbook) - §61.87(n)

### I.M.S.A.F.E (Fitness)
- **I**llness
- **M**edication
- **S**tress
- **A**lcohol - §91.17(a) / FOM 3.5.2
- **F**atigue
- **E**motion/eating

## A - AIRCRAFT
###  A.R.R.O.W+G (Document)
- **A**irworthiness - §91.203(b) cabin/cockpit
- **R**egistration - §91.203(a) *Initial = 7 yrs. (§47.40)*
- **R**adio license *(for cross-country)*
- **O**peration limitation - §91.9 *→ follow AFM*
- **W**eight and balance *→ AFM Ch.2, 6*
- *+*
- **G**1000 cockpit ref guide *→ G1000 STC Section 2*

### A.V.1.A.T.E.S (Maintenance/inspection)
- **A**nnual Inspection -  91.409(a) *→ every 12 months*
- **V**OR - §91.171 *→ every 30 days*
- **1**00 hours Inspection - §91.409(b) *→ no commercial flight*
- **A**irworthiness directives and life limit port - 91.403(a) *→ §39 (AD)*
- **T**transponder - §91.413(a)
- **E**LT inspection - §91.207
- **S**tatic and pitot system - §91.411

### Required Equipments
#### VFR (DAY) - §91.205(b)
- **A**ltimeter
- **T**achometer
- **O**il pressure gauge
- **M**anifold pressure gauge
- **A**ir speed indicator
- **T**emperature gauge
- **O**il temperature gauge
- **F**uel quantity gauge
- **L**anding gear position indicator
- **A**nti-collision light → *Small civil airplanes*
- **M**agnetic compass
- **E**LT
- **S**eat belt and shoulder harness

#### VFR (Night) - §91.205(c)
- **F**uses
- **L**anding light
- **A**nti-collision → *All U.S.-registered civil aircraft*
- **P**osition light
- **S**ource of electrical power

## V - enVironment - §91.103
- **N**OTAM
- **W**eather 
- **K**nown ATC delays
- **R**unway length
- **A**lternate airport
- **F**uel requirements
- **T**ake off and landing distance

## E - External factors 
- **R**esignation
- **A**nti authority
- **I**nvulnerability
- **I**mpulsivity
- **M**acho
